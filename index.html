Great Men of Royalty, Inc.

Great Men of Royalty, Inc. is a community-based organization that mentors young boys and young men through leadership development, life skills, etiquette, financial literacy, and positive role models—at no cost to families.

Our mission is to build confident, respectful, and purpose-driven young men by providing structured mentoring, workshops, and community-based support that strengthens families and schools.

About Great Men of Royalty

A brotherhood of mentors committed to building the next generation.

Vision

A community where every boy has access to strong male mentorship, positive identity, and the skills to become a confident leader—at home, at school, and in life.

Confidence Character Leadership Community

Mission

To mentor and empower young boys and young men through structured mentorship, leadership development, life-skills training, etiquette, financial literacy, and community service—creating pathways to success and purpose.

Programs

Great Men of Royalty programs include our full shared program menu (in partnership with Great Women of Royalty) for men, boys, youth, and families—delivered during the school day, after-school, evenings, weekends, and summer.

Royalty Mentoring (1:1 & Circles)

Positive male mentorship, accountability, and guidance for boys and young men.

Leadership Development

Confidence, communication, goal-setting, discipline, and decision-making.

Etiquette & Social Skills

Respect, manners, introductions, dining etiquette, and professional presence.

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, banking basics, earning, credit awareness, and smart choices.

Career Readiness & Workforce Prep

Resume basics, interview skills, workplace expectations, and career exploration.

Entrepreneurship & Business Basics

Idea-to-action workshops, youth entrepreneurship challenges, and guest speakers.

Academic Support & School Engagement

Motivation, attendance encouragement, tutoring referrals, and school partnership support.

Healthy Relationships & Conflict Resolution

Communication skills, peer pressure tools, anger management, and healthy boundaries.

Health, Wellness & Self-Care

Mental wellness conversations, self-esteem building, and healthy routines.

Community Service Projects

Service activities that build pride, teamwork, responsibility, and leadership in action.

Family Engagement & Parent Support

Parent sessions, family connection events, and supportive resources.

Youth Recognition & Awards

Celebrations that honor youth achievement, growth, leadership, and community impact.

Life Skills for Teens & Young Adults

Adulting basics: budgeting, planning, communication, time management, and independence.

Special Events & Enrichment

Workshops, guest speakers, leadership dinners, and positive community experiences.

Referral & Support Navigation

Connections to community resources, counseling supports, and partner services when needed.
